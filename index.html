About Us

Winning Nature In Charisma

Winning Nature in Charisma is a non governmental organisation for protecting and promoting the rights and dignity of persons with disabilities in Tanzania. Our work is focused on transforming mindsets towards disability to both persons with disabilities and persons without disabilities.

Our Mission

To create platforms where persons with disabilities will develop and show their potentials and abilities basing on their professions and passions.

Our Vission

To be an exemplary non-governmental organization that puts an emphasis on having a society that does not judge persons with disability basing on their disabilities.
